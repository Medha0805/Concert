About our band

Piyush Mishra is a multifaceted Indian artist who is an actor, singer, lyricist, playwriter, musician, and screenwriter. He leads ‘Ballimaaraan: The Piyush Mishra Project’, a dynamic musical ensemble that defies conventional genre boundaries. The band is renowned for its unique blend of emotive storytelling, eclectic melodies, and captivating performances, carving out a distinct niche in the music world.
